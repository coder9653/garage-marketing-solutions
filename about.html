About Garage Marketing Solutions

UK's leading digital marketing agency specializing in auto garage, tyre shop, and towing business marketing.

Our Story

Garage Marketing Solutions was founded in 2018 by a team of digital marketers who noticed a significant gap in the market. While working with various businesses, we realized that auto garages, tyre shops, and towing services were being underserved by generic marketing agencies.

We saw that these automotive businesses had unique challenges: seasonality, local competition, service-specific customer journeys, and the need for immediate bookings rather than long-term brand building.

So we decided to create an agency that specializes exclusively in automotive marketing. We built our systems from the ground up, testing and refining our strategies with real garages across the UK.

Our Mission

To help automotive businesses across the UK get more consistent, high-value bookings through proven digital marketing systems. We believe that every garage owner should be able to focus on what they do best—fixing cars—while we handle getting customers through the door.
