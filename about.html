Who We Are

Garage Marketing Solutions is the UK's leading digital marketing agency specializing exclusively in the automotive industry. We work with MOT centres, independent garages, tyre shops, and car servicing businesses across the United Kingdom.

Founded by automotive marketing specialists who understand the unique challenges of the garage industry, we've helped over 100 garages increase their bookings, improve their online visibility, and grow their revenue.

Unlike generic marketing agencies, we focus solely on automotive businesses. This means we understand MOT seasonality, how drivers search for garages, and what converts visitors into customers.

Our Mission

We're on a mission to help every independent garage in the UK compete with larger chains through smart digital marketing.

We believe that independent garages are the backbone of the UK automotive industry. They provide personalized service, fair prices, and build real relationships with their customers.

However, many struggle with marketing and online visibility. That's where we come in. We level the playing field by helping independent garages rank higher on Google, get more positive reviews, and attract more customers than their larger competitors.

Our success is measured by your success. When you get more MOT bookings, tyre jobs, and servicing appointments, we've done our job.
