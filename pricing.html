Pricing FAQs

Common questions about our pricing and services

Is there a setup fee?

+

Yes, there's a one-time setup fee of £299 for the Starter plan, £499 for the Growth plan, and £799 for the Premium plan. This covers initial strategy development, account setup, and campaign creation.

How is ad spend handled?

+

Ad spend is billed separately at cost. For example, if your plan includes £500 in ad spend, we'll add £500 to your monthly invoice. We never markup ad spend - you pay exactly what Google charges.

Can I cancel anytime?

+

After our 3-month guarantee period, all plans are month-to-month with a 30-day cancellation notice. There are no long-term contracts or cancellation fees.

Do you offer discounts for multiple locations?

+

Yes! If you have multiple garage locations, we offer volume discounts. Contact us for a custom quote based on the number of locations and services needed.
